CSPR Sentinel is a payment security and bilateral reputation platform for AI agent developers.

In this demo, an RWA diligence agent evaluates a tokenized commercial paper. The active policy defines an automatic payment limit, a daily budget, an allow list, and a minimum provider reputation.

The agent first purchases low-cost asset verification data. The request is within policy, so payment and delivery complete automatically. Next, the agent requests higher-cost risk intelligence. Because the price exceeds the automatic threshold, the request pauses before any signature is created.

The owner reviews the exact service, amount, and policy reason, then approves the purchase. The workflow resumes, records settlement evidence, receives the provider response, and completes the diligence report.

Every purchase appears in the audit ledger. Verified delivery, buyer ratings, payment reliability, and policy compliance update bilateral reputation. CSPR Sentinel is MCP-native, open source, and ready for Casper Testnet settlement.
